Assisted Living Community

Our Assisted living Staff assists residents with with activities of daily living.

Residents will receive three meals per day, snacks throughout the day, medication management, life enriching programs and outings, housekeeping, linen services, personal laundry service, and scheduled maintenance. Our caregivers provide 24/7 oversight and establish a unique service plan for every resident.

  • Three mails per day
  • Snacks and assorted beverages throughout the day
  • Medication administration
  • Trained staff available 24 hours per day
  • Nightly rounds catered to personal care needs
  • Bathing and dressing assistance
  • Weekly housekeeping
  • Personal laundry and linen service
  • Beds made daily
  • Activities 7 days per week
  • Assistance to all activities and meals
  • Emergency call system
  • All utilities except telephone
  • Satellite television (Television not provided)
  • Safety bars and shower bench in all bathrooms
  • Maintenance of rooms, grounds and landscaping
  • Trash pick-up
  • Fire suppression system
  • Full service beauty/barber shop
  • Wireless internet
