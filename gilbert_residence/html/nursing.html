Nursing Care Community

Nursing Care

Our nursing care community consists of specially designed rooms to accommodate a high level of care. Residents in our nursing unit may require assistance with activities of daily living or total care. The residents receive 3 meals per day, snacks throughout the day, daily programs, housekeeping, linen service, personal laundry service and 24/7 nurse oversight.

Our nursing care community provides a consistent level of assistance to maintain each residents unique care needs. Our registered nurses, licensed practical nurses and certified nursing assistants provide around the clock care. Our clinical team not only provides the necessary medical care they also care for the physical, spiritual and emotional well-being of each resident.

  • Care needs we Support:

  • Chronic illness
  • Neurological illness/disease
  • Loss of vision, speech or mobility due to progressive illness
  • Pulmonary disease (COPD and Emphysema)
  • Heart disease
  • Diabetes management
  • Alzheimer’s and dementia
  • Wound care
  • Hospice care

Hospice care is offered by select providers. Hospice provides an interdisciplinary treatment approach to end of life care that honors and upholds each individual’s wishes. Family, caregivers, and friends play a vital role in hospice care.
